Worth knowing about Montpellier: Psychology is not taught at Université de Montpellier (the one originally linked). It is taught at Université Paul-Valéry Montpellier 3 — a separate university — on the campus at Route de Mende, north of the city. All Montpellier options below are measured to that campus.

How to read this — and what to verify

  • Prices are the residences' advertised "from" rates, charges included (cc), and move with room type, floor and intake year — treat them as indicative and confirm the exact figure when booking.
  • Availability changes constantly and usually sits behind each booking portal, so it's marked "check" rather than guaranteed. Tell me Aina's intended start date and I can try to pull live availability.
  • Distances & times use each site's measured distance to the university; walking (~5 km/h) and cycling (~15 km/h) times are estimates. For the closest residences, the campus is genuinely a short walk, so public transport adds nothing.
  • Photos / virtual tours open on the official residence pages — I've linked galleries rather than copying images.
  • Reviews are summarised where they exist and noted as limited where they don't; they're a starting point, not a verdict.
  • Cheaper public backup: if Aina qualifies for a bourse, CROUS residences sit right by both campuses at roughly €250–410/month — say the word and I'll add them as a fallback tier.
